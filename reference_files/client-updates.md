## Free Guides — New Page at `/guides`

- All 12 checklists and reference cards from the Care Navigator toolkit now live at **whatsfrank.com/guides**.
- Families can select which checklist they need and view it directly on screen — no download required.
- Checklists are organized into **4 groups**:
  - **Tour Checklists** — for evaluating nursing homes, assisted living, memory care, and SNF rehab facilities
  - **Agency Evaluations** — home care, hospice, and CCRC financial review
  - **Reference Cards** — Medicare, resident rights, and financial planning
  - **Know My Parent** — the staff profile form, available to print directly
- Every checklist has a **Print** button. Families can print any checklist, write on it, and bring it to a tour or appointment.
- The guides are **completely free** — no account, no purchase required.
